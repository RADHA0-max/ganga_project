About Us

Nirmal Dhara was founded by a collective of environmental engineers, data scientists, and community leaders. We are dedicated to pioneering a new era of river conservation, starting with the sacred Ganga.

×

Our Mission

Our core mission is to restore the Ganga to its pristine state ('Nirmal Dhara') by integrating cutting-edge technology with community-driven initiatives. We aim to make water quality data transparent and accessible to everyone.
